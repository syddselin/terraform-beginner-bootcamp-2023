Finnish Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Butter or oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and then add the milk and vanilla extract (if using). Mix well.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until you have a smooth batter.
  4. Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add a small amount of butter or oil to coat the bottom.
  5. Pour a ladleful of batter into the pan and spread it out to form a thin pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges begin to brown, then flip the pancake and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more butter or oil to the pan as needed.
  8. Serve the Finnish pancakes hot with your favorite toppings, such as jam, fresh berries, or maple syrup.
